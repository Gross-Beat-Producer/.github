## 🚀 Summary
Gross Beat for Windows is an Image-Line effect plugin built on two editable envelopes — one for playback time and one for volume — that you draw to reshape audio as it plays. Loaded on a track in FL Studio, it turns loops, vocals, and one-shots into repeats, gates, rolls, and scratch-style edits that stay locked to your tempo. Everything is preset-driven, so Gross Beat lets you find rhythmic movement for a pattern and audition ideas in moments.

## ✨ Feature Set
1. **Time envelope** — Stretch, rewind, and pitch audio by shaping the playback timeline. ⏳
2. **Volume envelope** — Open and close gain to build gates, swells, and rhythmic chops. 🔊
3. **Scratch and pitch effects** — Combine both curves for scratches, rewinds, and pitch bends. 🎚️
4. **36 preset slots** — Save and recall complete moves and build your own effect library. 💾
5. **Tempo sync** — Snap every point to the FL Studio grid so effects stay in time. ⏱️

## 🎯 Getting Started
If you're new to how to use Gross Beat in FL Studio, start by adding it as an effect on the track that carries your audio. Play the pattern so sound flows through the plugin, then pick a time preset or a volume preset from the slots at the top and listen as it reshapes the loop. Fine-tune the curve points to dial in the stutter or gate, and automate the active slot so the effect evolves across your arrangement.
